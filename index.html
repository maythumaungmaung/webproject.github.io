About us

Breathe Fresh Air is to help parents of asthma kids who are currently living in Melbourne. Our webiste is for parents to manage and prevent asthma disorders in their children. The webite includes current status of (air quality, weather conditions), past 24 hrs information of (air quality, traffic volume) across Melbourne, and knowledge section. It also allows users to report the air quality related information.
